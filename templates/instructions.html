GAME OBJETIVE:

YOU HAVE TO ESCAPE FROM A MONSTER BY ANSWERING QUESTIONS WHILE YOU TRY TO KEEP THE MOST HEASRTS POSSIBLE...

HOW TO PLAY...

  1. You have to answer right a question so you can pass to the next bock
  2. If you don't get the question right you will lose half a heart
  3. if you lose all the hearts, the monster catches you and GAME OVER
